# Change Your Username 

![](../../assets/gifs/account-info.gif)

## What 
You can change your username at any time 

## How 
1. Click on your current **username** in the top right 
2. Click on **Account** 
3. Under **Basic Info**, input a new username 
4. Click **Save**
